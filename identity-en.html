K - Identity

I. K - Identity

- A company’s brand identity is how the business wants to be perceived by consumers.

- The components of the brand identity such as name, logo, tagline, typerface, are created by the business in an attempt to reflect the value that the company is trying to bring to the market and to appeal to its customers.

K – Identity – a branch of Module K – provides brand identity designing services to make your brand stand out from the others.

Our target is to help you design a brand identity that is a coherent articulation of your unique brand values, personality traits and aspirations.

This enables you to create a clearly definable position that connects with your customers, supporting you grow your businesses.

III. Workflow

Conduct Research

K Identity’s first priority is to understand our client, it’s mission, vision, and future challenges.

Strategize

Once the initial research is complete, we then concentrate on analysis, discovery, and clarity.

Combining all these elements with rational thinking and creative intelligence for the best strategy going forward.

Design & Develop

Now the really fun part starts. Using everything we have learnt from the beginning of our process, we integrate meaning with form to end with a refined design execution.

Deliver

The final stage for K Identity will be to manage assets and to create a platform to implement your new brand identity to full business launch and 3D dimension.
